Who We Are

The Lou-Von Family Foundation was a dream realized as James Wilson, Sr. saw a need for support, mentoring and change in the community.

The Lou-Von Family Foundation, whose name memorializes his beloved mother and sister, Louzetha Moore Wilson and Voncile Wilson respectively, was created on July 1, 2014 to serve as the major vehicle for the continuous delivery of support the numerous communities throughout the nation who have helped him to build the foundation of his success so that he would be able to be counted amoung those who can "give back" both throughout his lifetime and to continue to do so beyond his mortal life, by way of the organization he'd formed.

He found himself being constantly presented with families, communities, and people who are struggling under dire circumstances which prompted him to act so that there would be support for those in need through The Lou-Von Family Foundation, a federally exempt nonprofit corporation.
